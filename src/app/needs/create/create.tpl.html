Ask for a helping hand

What can I ask for?

Goods: What do you need? Food, clothing, emergency supplies, etc.

Housing: Do you need a place to stay? You can ask for short-term housing (less than 1 month), long term (less than 1 year), or permanent (roomate wanted).

Services: What can we do for you? Job search assistance, tasks/chores, counseling services, transportation, volunteers, etc.
